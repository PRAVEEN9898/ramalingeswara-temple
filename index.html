Temple Introduction

For over 400 years, Sri Ramalingeswara Swamy Temple has been the spiritual heart of Selapadu village. Dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, this sacred site has witnessed generations of devotion, festivals, and divine blessings.

Currently under reconstruction, the temple is being restored to its former glory while preserving its ancient architectural heritage. Your generous donations will help complete this sacred project.
