Temple Introduction

For over 400 years, Sri Ramalingeswara Swamy Temple has been the spiritual heart of Selapadu village. Dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, this sacred site has witnessed generations of devotion, prayers answered, and countless blessings bestowed upon devotees.

The temple is currently undergoing magnificent reconstruction to restore its ancient glory while incorporating modern facilities for devotees.
